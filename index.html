Goal:

Two players take turns guessing 5 letter words. Turn is incidated on each side of the game board.Each player's task is to find the hidden word. If a player guesses the word, they get a point. If neither player guesses by the guess limit, both loose out on that point. Whichever player wins the point goes first next round.

Each turn:

Enter a real 5-letter word. If the word is not in the dictionary it won't register. After entering a valid word, the letters will change color and give you (and your opponent) a hint for each letter you guessed.
